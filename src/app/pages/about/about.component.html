Eco planing проект

We have already said that the level of ecology in Kazakhstan is low, and in particular air pollution, lack of water, drought, garbage problem and many other environmental problems that harm the environment and our land.


Of course, the most important factor in solving these problems is the person. Because we are the creators of the major environmental problems mentioned above.

Because of humanity, the planet we live on is going to destruction, of course, this must be prevented. To do this, we need to keep the environment clean and tidy.

For this, as we know, various sweepings are held in the country, as soon as spring comes or on holidays, the state organize many sweepings. Actions will be held within the framework of nature conservation.
